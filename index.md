
# Privacy Policy for Flight Rights Tracker

**Last Updated: February 18, 2026**

## Overview

Flight Rights Tracker ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Email Address** (optional): When you choose to subscribe for updates, we collect your email address to send you app updates, flight rights tips, and feature announcements.
- **Flight Information**: Flight numbers, airports, and dates you enter to track your flights.

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: We do not collect device identifiers or usage analytics.
- **Location Data**: We do not collect or track your location.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Store and display your tracked flights within the app
- Send optional email updates about new features and flight rights information (only if you subscribe)
- Improve app functionality and user experience

## Data Storage

- **Flight Data**: Stored locally on your device using secure storage (AsyncStorage). This data never leaves your device.
- **Email Addresses**: Stored securely in an encrypted database on Railway.app servers located in the United States.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:
- **AviationStack API**: When you search for a flight, we send the flight number and date to AviationStack's API to retrieve real-time flight data. AviationStack's privacy policy applies to this data transmission.

We will never share your email address with third parties for marketing purposes.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View what data we have stored about you
- **Delete**: Request deletion of your email from our database at any time
- **Opt-Out**: Unsubscribe from emails at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at: rosenzweig.derek@gmail.com

## Data Retention

- **Flight Data**: Stored on your device until you delete the app or manually remove flights
- **Email Addresses**: Retained until you request deletion

## Children's Privacy

Flight Rights Tracker is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:
- HTTPS encryption for all network communications
- Encrypted database storage for email addresses
- No storage of sensitive personal or financial information

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **AviationStack**: Flight data API (https://aviationstack.com/privacy)
- **Railway.app**: Server and database hosting (https://railway.app/legal/privacy)

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via email (if subscribed) or in-app notification.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:
- Email: rosenzweig.derek@gmail.com
- App: Flight Rights Tracker

## Consent

By using Flight Rights Tracker, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
